How do I get a quote? +

Fill out the form below or call us at (806) 630-0278. We respond within a few hours to schedule a free on-site estimate.

Are you insured? +

Yes. Selah Spaces is fully licensed and insured. Your home and belongings are protected on every visit.

What's included in a deep clean? +

Everything — baseboards, appliances, inside cabinets, bathrooms, kitchen, bedrooms, and all high-touch surfaces. It's a full reset for your home.

Yes. Weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly plans available. Most recurring clients start with a deep clean, then switch to a maintenance plan.

How much does house cleaning cost in Lubbock? +

Pricing depends on your home's size, condition, and service type. We give every home a free on-site estimate so the price is accurate — no surprises.

Yes. We serve both English and Spanish-speaking clients throughout Lubbock. Use the language toggle at the top or just reach out in Spanish.
